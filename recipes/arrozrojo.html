Arroz Rojo

Tacos never dissapoint. This recepie was a favorite on the weekend dinners at home. It was easy delicious and always tastes good.

You can embelish this recepie with sour cream, lettuce and tomatoe. Just don't forget the salsa. It can make all the difference.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of Rice
  • 2 Big Tomatoes
  • 1 Garlic
  • 4 cups of Water
  • 1/4 cup of Oil
  • 1 tsp Salt
