Chilaquiles

The same salsa used for enchiladas is also used for chilaquiles. This makes it incrediblily easy to make on a weekday morning or dinner.

It contains all the popular flavors of a mexican meal.

  • 2 cups of Tortilla Chips
  • Enchilada Salsa
  • 1 Onion
  • 1/2 cup of Queso Fresco
  • 1/2 cup of Oil
