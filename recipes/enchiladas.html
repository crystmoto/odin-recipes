Enchiladas Recipes

Enchiladas with salsa roja.

I have fond memories of this recepie and wanted to share it with others. Writting this recepie allows me to relive the many moments I had. It included moments around the table helping prep the tortillas while I listened to my mothers and grandmothers converstaion about an uncle they miss.

While its a common recepie, I refraim from modifing it out of fear that I will loose that happy feeling I get while cooking.

Ingredients

  • Chile Guajillo
  • Water
  • Tortillas
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Queso Fresco
  • Sour Cream
